ABOUT

H E L L O

I am Noel Curtis, or GNKC on most platforms. I am currently a student at San Jose State University , it is my senior year and I will soon graduate with a BFA in Digital Media Art.

I am a multimedia artist who likes to mix the physical and digital. I have 3D printed models made in Blender and experimented with scanning objects to import into Blender. I like to work with code and I have experience with Javascript, Three.JS, and Arduino. I am always up to learning new things and working with new mediums.

Currently Working On...

I am working on my group DMA BFA Exhibition at Works / San José from November 14 - 25, 2025. My piece is called Proper Doll which includes physical and digital elements.
