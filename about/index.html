ABOUT

H E L L O

My full name is Gia-Noel Curtis. I was born in Hollister but was raised in San Jose for the majority of my life. I have always had a connection with art, even from a young age. I would draw on my homework often and was always stuck in my own head. Thankfully my middle and high schools gave opportunities for kids like me to be creative. I took beginner and advanced art classes in middle school along with an after-school comic book club. When I got into high school I sadly could only take a beginner's art class. However, my high school gave me the opportunity to go to Silicon Valley Career Technical Education (SVCTE or formally known as CCOC). That trade school had an animation program that helped me enter the digital art world. Without that opportunity, I wouldn’t have the experience of that side of art. These classes helped me become a better artist with multiple mediums. I as a current student at San Jose State University have gained a lot of skills and I hope to continue learning and improving as I continue school.
